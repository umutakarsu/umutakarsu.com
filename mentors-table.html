Mentors Table

The Genesis

I grew up navigating the world without a formal mentor. While others found solace in music during uncertain times, I found mine in the mental frameworks of specific thinkers. This project is the digital manifestation of the "navigation tool" I built for my own growth.

Why These Five?

These aren't just authors to me; they are the people I turn to when I need a different lens on reality:

  • Ray Dalio: For when I need to see the world as a machine and build a system that actually works.
  • Naval: For those moments I need to remind myself that true wealth is about freedom and leverage, not just status.
  • Robert Greene: To navigate the complex power dynamics of human nature without being naive.
  • Dan Koe: To stay focused on building a meaningful life and business in this digital noise.
  • Marcus Aurelius: My anchor for when things get chaotic and I need to return to my own center.

The Logic

This isn't a generic AI chatbot. It is a custom-prompted environment built on Claude, where each mentor operates within their documented worldviews. When I bring a problem to this "table," they don't give me comfort — they give me reality.
